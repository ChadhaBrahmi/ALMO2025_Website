Welcome to ALMO 2025

The Algerian Mathematical Olympiad (ALMO) is a prestigious national mathematics competition designed for high school students across Algeria. Organized annually, ALMO serves as a platform to identify and nurture the most talented young mathematicians in the country. It aims not only to promote mathematical excellence but also to inspire a deep appreciation for the beauty and rigor of mathematical thinking among students.

ALMO is held in alignment with the International Mathematical Olympiad (IMO) syllabus, covering key areas such as algebra, geometry, number theory, and combinatorics. The competition challenges students with creative, non-standard problems that test their problem-solving skills, logical reasoning, and mathematical ingenuity; well beyond the typical school curriculum.

Top-performing students in ALMO are selected for further training and may earn the opportunity to represent Algeria in prestigious international competitions such as the IMO, the Pan-African Mathematics Olympiad (PAMO), and other global math contests.

Through ALMO, Algeria strengthens its commitment to excellence in STEM education and contributes to the international mathematical community by cultivating a new generation of problem solvers, thinkers, and innovators.







Key aspects of the Algerian Mathematical Olympiad 2025

Selection Process

Students are initially selected based on their performance in mathematics during the school year, with a focus on those achieving a high average in their mathematics classes.

Training Camp

Selected students participate in a week-long training camp where they receive intensive training and problem-solving practice.

International Competitions

The Algerian team, selected through the Olympiad, will represent Algeria in international mathematical competitions like the International Mathematical Olympiad (IMO).

Algeria's Performance

In previous years, Algeria has achieved notable results in the IMO, including winning a gold medal at the 65th session held in the UK.

Ministry Support

The Ministry of National Education recognizes the importance of mathematics education and provides support for initiatives like the Algerian Mathematical Olympiad.

Focus on Mathematics Education

The Minister of National Education has emphasized improving the quality of mathematics education in Algeria, recognizing its crucial role in the country's future.

About the contest venue

The contest will be held at the National Mathematical High School, located in the heart of Kouba, Algiers. This venue is well-equipped with modern facilities, including spacious classrooms, dormitories, a canteen, and recreational areas. The school provides an ideal environment for students to focus on their studies and engage in collaborative learning.
